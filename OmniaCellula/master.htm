CellularMachines follows on from BioFundamentals and is designed to teach you the basics of cell biology - ranging from the types of cells to how these cells are organized and function, alone and within multicellular context.

This involves introducing you the common features of cell structure and then the molecular machines enable cells to perform the tasks required to be alive.

Throughout the readings, links will direct you to more detailed discussion (if you feel the need or the inclination), but the core material will focus on basic terms, concepts and cellular systems.

The readings will be linked into the True Knowledge™ assessment system, which will direct you back to materials that you have not yet completely mastered.
