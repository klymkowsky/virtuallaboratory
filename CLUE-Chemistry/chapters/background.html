Preface & Background:

The CLUE text, associated worksheets, beSocratic™ activities, and interactive Highlighter function are based one a coherent (and hopefully engaging) narrative.  They focus on the big ideas that underlie chemistry, specifically:

  • Models & theories
  • Scale (molecular & macroscopic)
  • Atoms & molecules
  • Structure
  • Energy & stability
  • Reactivity
  • Equilibrium
  • Entropy & probability
  • Free Energy

Each chapter includes explicit knowledge statements and performance expectations.
