With you mouse you will be able to move and rotated the two macromolecules into various positions.

start the movie by clicking "start" to get an idea

 

Questions to consider:

  • Why are there stabilizing (bonding) interactions between the two molecules?
  • Why are these stabilizing (bonding) interactions different when we change the orientation of the two molecules?
  • How would changes in molecular shapes influence the interactions between these two molecules?  
  • What factors determine the strongest interactions between the two molecules?
  • Is there a unique "best fit"?
  • How would minor changes in the structure of either protein alter the interaction strength?
  • How would flexibility in the molecules influence interaction strength?
