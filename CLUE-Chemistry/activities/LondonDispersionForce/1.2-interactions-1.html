Conservation
of energy at the atomic scale

You are looking at two Helium atoms.  The reset buttons can set them apart.

As the atoms approach one another, the potential/interaction energy (top) of the system and the kinetic energy (right side) of the atoms are displayed.

In this scene you cannot interact with the atoms.  

  

Questions to consider

  • Where does the energy come from to start that motion?
