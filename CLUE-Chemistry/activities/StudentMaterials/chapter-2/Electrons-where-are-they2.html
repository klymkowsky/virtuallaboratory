Lets add some data to our considerations:

The energy to remove the first (easiest) electron is called the first ionization energy; the energy required to remove a second electron called the second ionization energy.  

For Mg (g) the first ionization energy is 738 kJ/mol,
while for calcium it is 590 kJ/mol. 

Mg’s second ionization energy is 1450 kJ/mol.
