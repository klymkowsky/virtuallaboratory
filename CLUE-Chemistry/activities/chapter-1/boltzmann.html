Boltzmann & molecular motion

Building on the ideas of Dalton, Avogadro, and others, Maxwell and Boltzmann developed a theory that describes the molecular motions in gases.

At a particular temperature, the average kinetic energy of the molecules are equal, no matter what the gas. That said, the molecules are not all moving with the same velocity - some are moving very fast, others very slowly.
