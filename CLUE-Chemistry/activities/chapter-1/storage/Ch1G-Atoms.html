Stable interactions and energy conservation: As you may have noted, the interacting atoms oscillate changing potential energy into kinetic energy and then back again in what appears to be a perpetual motion machine.

You can modify the oscillations by removing energy through a collision with another atom in the system (try removing energy with the "lose energy" button).
