Potential energy and systems:  Systems can have potential energy.  Where there is potential energy, it can be converted into other types of energy, such as kinetic energy.   

An isolated atom has zero potential energy. 

In this applet, you can use your cursor to move the (helium) atoms.

Use your cursor so that the atoms are isolated from one another.
