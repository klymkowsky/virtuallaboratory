Conventional teaching all too often accepts memorization and pattern recognition as true learning.  Both students and teachers can mistake this for subject mastery.

To gain a rigorous understanding one needs to recognize, work with, & apply empirical data and abstract concepts to real-life scenarios.

Through our virtuallaboratory and SocraticChat projects our goal was to help students recognize, confront, correct, and expand their understanding of scientific ideas and techniques: cyberlearning video
