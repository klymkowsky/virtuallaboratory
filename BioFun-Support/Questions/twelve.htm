That is a good working definition of a species?

A particular type of organism
An interbreeding population
A group of organisms that live in the same area

What is meant by reproductive isolation?

Related populations are no longer able to interbreed successfully
Two populations live in different regions
Organisms reproduce in private

What is meant by a 'living fossil'?

An organism made of stone
 A population of organism that has not changed dramatically over long periods of time
Organisms that have extremely long lifetimes

Why are most species short-lived?

The environment is constantly changing
 Species, like organisms, have a defined natural life spans

Disruptive selection

  1. tends to break a population into subpopulations that utilize different resources
  2. tends to increases the competition between individuals for the same resources
  3. leads species to merge together

What is geographical isolation?

  1. The breaking of a population into distinct groups by a physical barrier
  2. The seperation of populations over time
  3. The processes responsible for all speciation.

Even if the physical environment were to stay constant, populations would still change over time because

  1. organisms always evolve, no matter what
  2. other organisms in the environment are evolving, and this produces new selective pressures
  3. entropy
