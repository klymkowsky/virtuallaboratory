The best use of the SocraticBioBot is to ask questions such as:

Is my answer [here] to this question [here] correct or adequate and if not what is wrong with it?

You can also ask what key concepts are missing or misapplied in your answer.

 

If you just want an answer, google it. If you want to understand what is going on, talk with us. 
If you discover a response that seems incorrect, please let us know; it helps us if you send one or two good references on the topic.
